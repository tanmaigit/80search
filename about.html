About 80Search

Welcome to 80Search, your personal search portal designed to simplify your web browsing experience.

What We Do

80Search provides a clean, fast, and distraction-free interface to search the web using your preferred search engine. Whether you prefer Yahoo or Google, we make it easy to find what you're looking for without the clutter.

Our Mission

In a world filled with distractions, we believe that search should be simple, fast, and focused. 80Search is built with these principles in mind:

  • Simplicity: A clean interface with no unnecessary features
  • Speed: Lightning-fast search that gets you to your results instantly
  • Privacy: We don't track your searches or store your data
  • Choice: Use your preferred search engine with ease

Features

  • Quick search with Yahoo or Google
  • Keyboard shortcuts (Press Enter to search)
  • Clean, modern interface
  • Mobile-friendly design
  • No ads, no tracking, no bloat

Get Started

Simply type your query in the search box, select your preferred search engine, and press Enter. It's that easy!
